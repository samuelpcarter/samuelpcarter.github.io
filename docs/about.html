About Me

Hi, I'm Sam Carter, a lab technician and robotics instructor from Carlsbad, California. At GKN Additive I managed SLA, PolyJet, and FDM printers for custom manufacturing.

I've coordinated robotics programs with the Carlsbad Educational Foundation and mentored dozens of student teams. Outside the lab I enjoy hiking and supporting community STEM outreach.
